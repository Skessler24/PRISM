🌍 CLD Guidance — Enrich Section 8

💡 Help Assist: CLD (Culturally and Linguistically Diverse) students require non-biased assessments conducted in their primary language whenever possible. An interpreter must be arranged for all meetings. Consider cultural background in evaluation — consult with a bilingual specialist when available.
